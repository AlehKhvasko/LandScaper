Game Rules

You are starting a landscaping business, but all you have are your teeth.


Using just your teeth, you can spend the day cutting lawns and make $1.


You are currently using your teeth, you can buy a pair of rusty scissors for $5.


Using the rusty scissors, you can spend the day cutting lawns and make $5.


You can buy an old-time push lawnmower for $25.


You can spend the day cutting lawns and make $50.


You can buy a fancy battery-powered lawnmower for $250.


You can hire a team of starving students for $500.


Using the the team of starving students, you can spend the day cutting lawns and make $250.


You win the game when you have a team of starving students and $1000.
